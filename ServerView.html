LED Dance Platform Mode Interface

Check one box then submit to change the mode of the LED Dance Platform.

Checking more than 1 box and submitting will result to default mode which is random squares that do not change.
Random squares not changing
Random squares that change to music
Random squares that change to music with a ring on edge that changes to bass
Visualizer that emanates out from the center
Power levels of different ranges in the music
Displays Theta then a Chi to the bass of the music
