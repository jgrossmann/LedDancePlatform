LED Dance Platform Mode Interface

Check one box then submit to change the mode of the LED Dance Platform.

Each one shows the mode as it is specified in the software, and a description of each.
"randsquares": Random squares not changing

"randsquares,change=True": Random squares that change to music

"bassring": Random squares that change to music with a ring on edge that changes to bass

"visualize1": Visualizer that emanates out from the center

"levels": Power levels of different ranges in the music

"thetachi": Displays Theta then a Chi to the bass of the music
